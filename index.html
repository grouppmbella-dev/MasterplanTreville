Workload by Manpower

Each task's Manpower field is grouped together. Total days = sum of each task's Plan period (Plan Finish − Plan Start + 1), so a high number may mean either a long task or several overlapping tasks for that person/team.

Version History / Recovery

The app keeps recent snapshots of your data automatically (about every 5 minutes while you're working, plus right before risky actions like "Load Sample" or deleting a row). If something gets deleted by mistake, restore an earlier version here — your current data is snapshotted first, so you can always undo a restore too.
